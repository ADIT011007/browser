Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I install Eagle Eye Browser?

Answer: The browser is currently only available on Windows. You can download the zip file, extract it, and then run the setup.

Question 2: Can I import bookmarks or settings from my previous browser?

Answer: No, you can't import bookmarks or settings from any browser. This feature might get implemented in the future.

Question 3: Does Eagle Eye Browser support browser extensions?

Answer: No, browser extension support is not available. However, custom handmade extensions made by the developer exclusively for Eagle Eye Browser are in development.

Question 4: How do I enable private mode in Eagle Eye Browser?

Answer: To enable private mode, simply open the advanced settings page and turn on private mode.

Question 5: Is my data secure while using Eagle Eye Browser?

Answer: While the browser does not collect any data, websites might. That's why we recommend using private mode to enhance your privacy and security.

Question 6: How often are updates released for Eagle Eye Browser?

Answer: We strive to provide regular updates to Eagle Eye Browser to ensure optimal performance, security, and compatibility. Updates are typically released on a monthly basis, but may vary depending on the nature of the update.
