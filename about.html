Welcome to Egale Eye Browser

A labor of love crafted by a solo developer passionate about providing users with a browsing experience that prioritizes privacy, speed, and efficiency. Built specifically for low-end Windows systems, Egale Eye Browser delivers impressive performance without compromising on simplicity.

Privacy is Paramount

As a solo developer, I understand the importance of safeguarding your personal information. That's why Egale Eye Browser, built using Google's Chromium Embedded Framework (CEF) and other carefully chosen libraries, never collects any data from its users, ensuring your privacy remains intact and your browsing activities stay confidential.

Experience the Speed

Optimized for efficiency, our browser allows you to navigate the web swiftly and smoothly, even on low-end Windows systems. Say goodbye to sluggish browsing and hello to a seamless online experience.

Streamlined User Interface

Our streamlined user interface, powered by Google's CEF, provides a clutter-free browsing environment, making it easy to focus on what matters most: the content you're browsing. And stay tuned for upcoming UI enhancements that will make your browsing experience even more enjoyable.
